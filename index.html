Copa America 2021

Copa America 2021 is over, and Argentina is the queen of South America after 28 years, with Messi finally winning his first international title. It was a long journey with 4 group matches and three knock-out matches.After rejecting the invitation from CONMEBOL, Qatar and Australia forced the competition to be formed of two groups of 5 teams each. Argentina win the Copa America title for the first time since 1993 as they beat Brazil 1-0 in the final at the historic Maracana Stadium in Rio de Janeiro. Angel Di Maria scored the only goal of the match in the 22nd minute. This is Lionel Messi's first major trophy with the Argentina senior team.
